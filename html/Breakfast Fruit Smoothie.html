Breakfast Fruit Smoothie

Source: Eatthismuch.com

Ingredients

1/2 Cup strawberry

1/2 Banana

1/2 Cup OJ

Directions

Just put all the ingredients in the blender and blend until smooth. Optionally, blend with ice. Then drink and enjoy!
