Zhou Brownies

Ingredients

2 sticks unsalted butter (225g)

1 tbsp espresso powder (6g)

Coffee ice cube (30g)


4 eggs

1 tbsp vanilla paste (15g)

2 1/4 cups white sugar (450g)


1/2 cup dutch-processed cocoa powder (45g)

1 tbsp espresso powder (6g)

1 tbsp kosher salt (9g)


8 oz melted 75% cacao chocolate (225g)

1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour (215g)


3.5 oz chopped milk chocolate (100g)

3.5 oz chopped 75% cacao chocolate (100g)

6 oz chopped 75% cacao chocolate, for topping (170g)

Directions

Brown Butter
Take off heat
Add in Espresso Powder
Add in Coffee ice cube

In a separate container break in 4 eggs
Add in vanilla and sugar
Beat until fluffy
Sift in Cocoa, Espresso and salt

Mix in the butter
Add in melted chocolate
Sift Flour

Chop Milk and dark chocolate
Pour batter into tray
Put some chocolate bits on top

Wrap tray(33x23) in plastic wrap and rest in fridge for 3 days

Bake at 180 for 30 mins

Seal with foil and put into freezer for 30 minutes
Rest in fridge for 24 hours
