Cheesy Garlic Rolls

By: Sage Bakes
  • 12 rolls
Ingredients
  • Dough
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • Filling
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup mozzarella
Directions
Make the dough
Mix the flour and milk.
Knead until smooth.
Shape and bake
Fill and roll the dough.
Bake until golden.
