# Burger Buns V2

## Ingredients

- **119**g Water
- **114**g Milk
- **9**g Yeast
- **550**g Bread Flour
- **14**g Sugar
- **5**g Salt
- **43**g Vegetable Oil
- **1** Egg
- **1** Egg Yolk

## Directions

1. Mix Water Milk and Yeast
  Mix Dry ingredients
  Add in Oil
  Add in Eggs
  Add in Yeast
2. Rise 1 Hour
  Divide into 6
  Rise for 30 mins in tray
  Brush with egg wash
3. 190C for 15 mins
  Brush with butter
