# Pancakes

## Ingredients

- **2** cups flour
- **1/2** Tbsp baking powder
- **1/2** tsp baking soda
- **1/4** tsp salt
- **1/2** tsp vanilla
- **5** Tbsp oil
- **1** tsp sugar
- **2** eggs
- **2** cups milk
- Just mix the dry and the wet separately, then combine and whisk until there are no more lumps. Half the time I just do it all in one bowl and it still turns out perfectly!
