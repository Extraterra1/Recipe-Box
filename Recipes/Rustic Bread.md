# Rustic Bread

## Ingredients

- Poolish
- **150**g water
- **150**g AP Flour
- Pinch of yeast
- Dough
- **280**g water 37C
- **2**g yeast
- **400**g AP flour
- **50**g Whole wheat flour
- **10**g Salt

## Directions

1. Mix and rest for 30 mins
  Fold and rest for 30 mins
  Fold and rest for 60 mins
  Shape and rest on proofing basket for 30-50mins
