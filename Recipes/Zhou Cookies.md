# Zhou Cookies

## Ingredients

- **Toffee:**
- **1** stick unsalted butter
- **1** cup light brown sugar
- **1** tsp kosher salt
- **Brown Butter:**
- **2** sticks unsalted butter
- **2** ice cubes
- **Dough:**
- **1/2** cup sugar
- **1 ½** cups dark brown sugar
- **2** tsp espresso powder
- **2** tsp kosher salt
- **½** tsp baking soda
- **2** eggs
- **2** tsp good vanilla extract
- **2 ½** cups all purpose flour
- **6** oz chocolate, 70% cacao minimum
- Maldon sea salt
