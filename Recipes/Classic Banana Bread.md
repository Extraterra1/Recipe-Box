# Classic Banana Bread

Source: [M.youtube.com](https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=2RoUoMnDHlc)

## Ingredients

- ▪560g (6 large) very ripe bananas
- ▪115g or 1 stick butter, melted
- ▪3 large eggs
- ▪120g or 2/3c dark brown sugar
- ▪70g or 1/4c molasses
- ▪5g or 1tsp vanilla extract
- ▪140g or 1/2c sour cream
- ▪40g or 3Tbsp olive oil
- ▪420g or 3 1/4c ap flour
- ▪12g or 2tsp salt
- ▪15g or 1Tbsp baking powder
- ▪6g or 1 1/4tsp baking soda

## Directions

1. Mash bananas. Add eggs, brown sugar, molasses, vanilla, sour cream, and melted (but not super hot) butter. Whisk to combine.
2. In a separate bowl, combine flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda. Whisk to combine then fold dry ingredients into wet. Don’t overmix.
3. Spray or butter a loaf 1.5lb (10”x5”x3”) loaf pan then prepare with a sling of parchment. Spray or butter parchment then transfer banana bread mix into the pan. See recipe below if using a 1lb loaf pan.
4. Tap to level then bake at 350F/175C for 60-70min.
