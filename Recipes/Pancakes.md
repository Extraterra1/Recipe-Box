# Pancakes

## Ingredients

- **1 ½** cups all-purpose flour
- **3** teaspoons baking powder
- **2** tablespoons white sugar
- **¼** teaspoon salt
- **1 ½** cups milk
- **3** tablespoons butter, melted (42.5g)
- **1** large egg
- **1** teaspoon vanilla extract

## Directions

1. **Directions:**
2. 1. Whisk together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a large bowl.
3. 2. In another bowl, mix milk, melted butter, egg, and vanilla.
4. 3. Pour wet ingredients into dry and stir until just combined.
5. 4. Let batter rest for 5–10 minutes.
6. 5. Heat a skillet over medium heat and lightly grease.
7. 6. Pour ¼ cup batter per pancake into skillet.
8. 7. Cook until bubbles form and edges set, then flip and cook until golden.
9. 8. Serve warm.
