# Vanilla Cake

## Ingredients

- **1 ½** cups (225 g) plain flour / all-purpose flour
- **1** tablespoon (16 g) baking powder
- **1** cup (240 g) caster sugar / superfine sugar
- **180** g (¾ cup / 6.5 oz) butter, melted
- **1 ½** cups (375 mL) milk
- **2** teaspoon (10 g) vanilla extract

## Directions

1. Preheat the oven to 160°C / 320°F (fan/convection) or 180°C / 355°F (standard). Grease and line a 20 cm (8 inch) round cake tin with baking paper.
2. In a large mixing bowl, sift together plain flour and baking powder. Whisk in sugar. Make a well in the centre of the dry ingredients. Add melted butter, milk and vanilla essence. Whisk altogether until well combined (the butter may solidify and make the batter a bit lumpy - this is okay).
3. Pour into the prepared tin. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until golden and a skewer inserted in the centre of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool in the tin for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
