# Breakfast Fruit Smoothie

Source: [Eatthismuch.com](https://www.eatthismuch.com/recipe/nutrition/breakfast-fruit-smoothie,34670/)

## Ingredients

- **1/2** Cup strawberry
- **1/2** Banana
- **1/2** Cup OJ

## Directions

1. Just put all the ingredients in the blender and blend until smooth. Optionally, blend with ice. Then drink and enjoy!

## Nutrition

- Carbs 66g
  Fat
  Protein 4g
  Fiber
  Sodium 13mg
  Cholesterol 0mg
